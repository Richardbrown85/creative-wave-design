Bonner's Bazaar store logo

  • Bazaar means a large shop selling miscellaneous goods.
  • Brown bag focuses on handcrafted items, a brown bag logo can help convey the natural, artisanal quality of the products.
The idea behind this logo was a brown bag to represent the focus on hancrafted items which Bonners Bazaar produce and sell. They offer a full range of miscellaneous goods such as customised water bottles, key rings, notepad covers and so on. As bazaar stands for market, a large shop selling miscellaneous goods a bold and strong font was chosen.

Mytholmroyd Community Train Station logo

  • Two green leaves symbolise life, growth, connection and nature.
  • Two figures joined to each other symbolise community and unity.
  • The train station sign rotated 90 degrees form the two figures.
The idea behind this concept is two figures to represent the community as they are joined in one. Leaves generally symbolise unity, connection and nature. Community and collaboration. Working together towards a common goal. The colour scheme was chosen for nature, warmth and comfort.

Adamah contracting LTD logo

  • The word Adamah in Hebrew means ground or earth.
  • When you think about earth - water, soil and sky.
  • Three main colours are green, brown and blue.
This logo design was created to resemble both ground and earth in a eye-catching simplistic form of the three main colours that people associate with the earth - green, brown and blue. The brown being the greater colour for the ground.
